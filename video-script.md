## **TECHNICAL NOTES FOR RECORDING:**

### **Recording Setup:**
- Use screen recording software (OBS, Camtasia, or similar)
- Set resolution to 1920x1080 or higher
- Record at 30fps for smooth playback
- Use a good quality microphone for clear audio

### **Recording Tips:**
- Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
- Pause briefly between sections for natural flow
- Use smooth mouse movements
- Highlight important elements with cursor
- Keep the recording window clean and professional
